The Best Movies According to Ram Janak

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Widely considered one of the best films ever made, it tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly imprisoned for murder, and his friendship with Red. The movie is slow but deeply rewarding, filled with themes of hope, resilience, and redemption. Morgan Freeman’s narration is unforgettable.

Your Name (Kimi no Na wa, 2016)

A breathtakingly animated Japanese film by Makoto Shinkai. It follows two teenagers who mysteriously swap bodies and slowly develop a connection that transcends time and space. The visuals are stunning, the soundtrack is emotional, and the story blends romance, humor, and tragedy beautifully.

Interstellar (2014)

Christopher Nolan’s epic explores love, time, and survival as astronauts travel through a wormhole to save humanity. With mind-blowing visuals of space, emotional father-daughter moments, and a scientifically inspired storyline, it mixes science with deep emotions. Hans Zimmer’s organ-based score is haunting.
